Q1 2026 focuses on three strategic pillars: **improving lawyer efficiency**, **scaling operations through automation**, and **increasing customer satisfaction**. #### Business Objectives & Product Outcomes

**1. Improve Lawyer Efficiency**
- **Business Outcomes**:
  - Increase # of avg. cases handled per lawyer per month
  - Reduce avg. end-to-end case handling time
  - Reduce avg. time spent on context gaining
- **Product Outcomes** (Lawyer Efficiency Squad):
  - Reduce median time from case opened → 1st action created
  - Reduce email draft time to <5 minutes
  - Reduce document draft time to <5 minutes
  - 30% of summary-suggested actions executed
  - 30%+ of app-suggested next steps executed within 24 hours

**2. Scale Business Without Increasing Headcount** (AI Squad)
- **Business Outcomes**:
  - Decrease inbound document processing time from 5.6 days → <12 hours
  - 70%+ of documents processed via automated workflow
  - Reduce Post team hours spent on classification/routing
  - Increase documents processed per Post FTE to ≥1.0
- **Product Outcomes**:
  - <10% manual correction rate for auto-classified documents
  - 50% of Post staff accept auto-classification without review

**3. Scale Business - Finance Automation**
- **Business Outcomes**:
  - Reduce Finance team hours on invoice creation
  - Increase invoices processed per Finance team member
  - 90% of AURE cases fully auto-invoiced
- **Product Outcomes**:
  - <30 mins from "eligible for invoicing" → invoice draft created
  - 90% of AURE auto-invoiced without manual correction

**4. Increase Customer Satisfaction** (Customer Trust Squad)
- **Business Outcomes**:
  - Reduce total complaint rate to <1%
  - Reduce lawyer-caused complaints to <0.7%
  - Increase lawyer NPS to >65
- **Product Outcomes**:
  - 30%+ of active cases receive ≥1 update in last 7 days
  - 80% of client inquiries answered within 24 hours
  - 60%+ of application prompts result in client update within 24 hours

## Dependencies & Risks

### Cross-Initiative Dependencies

**AI Infrastructure Dependencies**
- OS-2831 (Lawyer AI) and OS-2713 (Document AI) likely share AI/ML infrastructure
- Need coordination on model deployment, monitoring, and scaling

**Platform Migration Impact**
- OS-2249 (CF Migration) affects multiple teams and could impact other initiatives
- Risk of disruption during migration period

**Integration Dependencies**
- OS-1511 (E-Consult/Webakte) affects document workflows
- May impact OS-2713 (Document Processing) if not coordinated

**Data/KPI Dependencies**
- OS-2477 (KPI Tracking) needs to be in place to measure success of other initiatives
- Should be prioritized to enable data-driven decisions

### Key Risks

**1. AI Adoption Risk** 🔴
- **Risk**: Lawyers may not trust or adopt AI-suggested actions
- **Mitigation**: Start with high-confidence suggestions, build trust gradually
- **Impact**: Could delay achieving 30% execution rate targets

**2. Automation Quality Risk** 🟡
- **Risk**: Auto-classification/invoicing accuracy below targets
- **Mitigation**: Phased rollout, human-in-the-loop for edge cases
- **Impact**: May require more manual intervention than planned

**3. Integration Complexity** 🟡
- **Risk**: External integrations (E-Consult, beA, Aircall) more complex than expected
- **Mitigation**: Early technical spikes, vendor engagement
- **Impact**: Timeline delays, potential scope reduction

**4. Change Management** 🟡
- **Risk**: Resistance to platform migration and new workflows
- **Mitigation**: Strong communication, training, phased rollout
- **Impact**: Slower adoption, reduced efficiency gains

**5. Resource Constraints** 🟡
- **Risk**: 9 active epics with limited team capacity
- **Mitigation**: Clear prioritization, accept some initiatives may slip
- **Impact**: Not all Q1 goals achieved
