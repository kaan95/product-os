**Last Updated**: 2026-04-14
**Initiative Path**: `.claude/product/initiatives/2026/Q2/customer-excellence/premium-package/`

---

## Overview

The Premium Package initiative aims to increase Legalhero's take rate from partner lawyers by introducing a tiered Basis/Premium model. Instead of a hard price increase (rejected due to churn risk), lawyers voluntarily opt into Premium in exchange for prioritized case access and AI efficiency tools. The primary hook for Phase 1 is prioritized access to high-value cases (especially Kündigungen). Phase 2 (from September) relies on measurable AI efficiency gains. Pilot launch is targeted for **1 May 2026** with 10 pilot law firms.

## Insights & Learnings

- [2026-02-26] Core lawyer motivation is purely financial: "Anwälte machen es nur, wenn am Ende finanziell mehr Geld bei ihnen ankommt." AI features and efficiency do not sell; case access and money do — source: Feb 26 meeting
- [2026-02-26] 60% of total revenue comes from court-based labor law cases (gerichtliches Arbeitsrecht) — source: Feb 26 meeting
- [2026-02-26] Currently >70% of incoming cases are being accepted; oversupply of partners makes Kündigungen scarcity a credible hook — source: Feb 26 meeting
- [2026-03-26] ~240 Kündigungen cases/month (150–180 go to court). Sufficient volume to make Premium attractive for 20–25 law firms max before the hook dilutes — source: Mar 26 meeting
- [2026-03-26] Muno example: currently earns ~13 EL cases; needs only 7 lawsuits at premium share to break even — lawyers can be shown a clear business case — source: Mar 26 meeting
- [2026-04-01] Lawyer communication should focus on "mehr Geld durch bessere Fälle" — avoid "trial" branding; simply explain that first payout is at old conditions — source: Apr 1 meeting
- [2026-04-08] Monthly fixed fee (~20,000 EUR at 100 lawyers × 200 EUR) has negligible financial impact but good investor optics; decided against for now due to complexity — source: Apr 8 meeting

## Premium Value Proposition (as of April 2026)

**Phase 1 Hook (May–September 2026): Case Access**
- Premium CTL view: 1 hour early access to new cases before non-premium lawyers
- Exclusive "Premium cases" (marked with star): high-value Kündigungen, payment cases, accident cases — only every 10th–15th qualifying case to avoid diluting lead quality
- Higher case acceptance limits (manual adjustment)
- Prioritized billing & faster payout
- Prioritized coverage requests (Deckungsanfragen)
- Prioritized case intake
- Prioritized partner management

**Phase 2 (from September 2026): AI Efficiency**
- AI features available first and unlimited (Basis plan gets token-limited access)
- AI summaries (email + case file)
- Action proposals (next step suggestions)
- Document generation (e.g., Klageschriften)
- AI to-do list for organizing the lawyer's day
- BIO in back office
- Possible: beA integration
