## Detailed Opportunities

### Parent: Partner Lawyer Responsiveness & Communication

**Type**: Problem
**Stakeholder**: Clients (via RSV escalation)
**Description**: Partner lawyers fail to communicate with clients in a timely and professional manner. This is the single largest driver of complaints across all three months of Q1 — 62 of 95 PA-attributed complaints (65%) relate to "Keine Rückmeldung" or "Kommunikation." Clients lose trust, seek lawyers elsewhere, and RSV partners escalate to Legalhero. The pattern is persistent and shows no improvement trend.

| Dimension | Rating | Evidence |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Pain Level | High | Clients feel abandoned, threaten to cancel mandates, seek alternative lawyers. RSV trust eroded. Multiple cases escalated to RSV Vorstände. |
| Reach | Broad | PA-attributed fault in 95 of 228 complaints (42%). Keine Rückmeldung alone = 39 complaints. Spans all RSVs and multiple Kanzleien. |
| Frequency | Constant | Identical pattern every month: Jan ~31, Feb ~36, Mar ~28 PA-fault cases. No improvement. |
| **Score** | **27** | |

**Supporting Evidence**:
> "Seit 9 Monaten keine Rückmeldung. Vertrauen vollständig erschüttert. Droht mit Beendigung der Zusammenarbeit."
> — Pauline Schreiter, ARAG complaint

> "Kommunikation unzureichend. Auf Anfrage mit Fristsetzung nicht reagiert, keine Info zum Sachstand, Eindruck, dass Interessen nicht konsequent vertreten werden."
> — Zsanett Harkai, HUK complaint

> "Läuft schon 1,5 Jahre - keine Info über Sachstand"
> — Martin Link, HUK complaint

> "Der Kunde habe mitgeteilt, dass er den zuständigen Rechtsanwalt seit längerer Zeit nicht erreichen könne. Er habe angegeben, dass er seit zwei Wochen keinerlei Rückmeldung mehr erhalten habe."
> — Vincent Günther, ARAG complaint

> "hiermit wende ich mich erneut mit einer dringenden Beschwerde... Mangelnde Kommunikation: Weder auf E-Mails noch auf Telefonate erhalte ich zeitnahe Rückmeldungen. Enorme Verzögerungen: Ein Schreiben der Gegenseite vom 08.01.2026 wurde mir erst am 26.02.2026 weitergeleitet."
> — Bernd Hofscheier, Deurag complaint

**Where it occurs**: Post-mandate assignment, throughout entire case lifecycle
**Current workaround**: Clients call RSV to escalate → RSV sends complaint to LH → LH contacts PA. Purely reactive cycle.
**Strategic relevance**: Directly threatens Pillar 3 (Process Standardization & Quality Assurance), Risk 1 (RSV Partner Churn), and Risk 2 (Lawyer Supply-Side Quality Issues)

### Parent: Proactive Case Oversight

**Type**: Need
**Stakeholder**: Internal (operations), Clients (indirect)
**Description**: Legalhero currently relies on RSV complaints as the primary trigger to discover that a partner lawyer is neglecting a case. There is no proactive system to detect stalled cases, missed SLAs, or unresponsive lawyers before the client escalates. This is the root cause behind most PA-related complaints. By the time a complaint arrives, client trust is already broken and LH appears complicit.

| Dimension | Rating | Evidence |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Pain Level | High | Reactive posture means every PA failure becomes a client relationship loss. LH appears negligent. Directly causes high-severity cases. |
| Reach | Broad | Affects the entire case portfolio. All 95 PA-fault complaints in Q1 represent cases where earlier detection could have prevented escalation. |
| Frequency | Constant | Every undetected stalled case is a missed intervention. |
| **Score** | **27** | |

**Supporting Evidence**:
> Heiko Florschütz — offboarded lawyer managed case for 1.5+ years without LH oversight, until client publicly asked for help. (Beschwerdelevel: 3 - Hoch)

> "VN wartet vergeblich auf Anruf des RA. Fall 23.02.26 übergeben... Telefontermin vereinbart, niemand hat angerufen."
> — Gerhard Manzeschke, Deurag (Grund LH: PO - Kontrolle)

> Stefan Emmer — PA left case unattended until Ausschlussklausel made claims unenforceable. Quality team didn't catch it. (Beschwerdelevel: 3 - Hoch)

**Where it occurs**: Throughout case lifecycle, especially post-mandate-assignment
**Current workaround**: RSV complaint → LH reacts → contacts PA. Purely reactive.
**Strategic relevance**: Core enabler for Pillar 3 (Quality Assurance) and Pillar 4 (Data-Driven Infrastructure). Automated case-health signals would prevent the majority of PA complaints before they happen.

| Dimension | Rating | Evidence |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Pain Level | Medium | Prevents data-driven quality management. Cannot trend-analyze or prioritize effectively. Cannot hold specific partners accountable. |
| Reach | Broad | Affects all 228 Q1 complaints and the entire historical dataset. |
| Frequency | Constant | Structural gap — worsening, not improving. |
