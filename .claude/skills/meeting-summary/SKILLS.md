# Meeting Summary Skill

Summarize the provided meeting notes into two sections:

1. **Summary** — 3–5 bullet points capturing the key discussion points and decisions, grouped by theme.
2. **Action Items** — a numbered to-do list of concrete next steps, each with an owner (if mentioned) and due date (if mentioned).

Keep the output concise. No filler. If the notes are unclear, infer intent from context.
